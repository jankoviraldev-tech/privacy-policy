Privacy Policy – MyBite

Last updated: 18.03.2026

MyBite (“we”, “our”, “app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the app:

  • Account information (such as email, if you create an account)
  • Food images you upload for analysis
  • Usage data (such as device type and app interactions)

2. How We Use Information

We use your data to:

  • Analyze food images and provide nutritional insights
  • Operate, improve, and maintain the app
  • Ensure security and prevent misuse

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • OpenAI API – to analyze food images and generate nutrition data
  • Firebase (Google) – for authentication, database, and app infrastructure

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is securely stored using Firebase services
  • We take reasonable measures to protect your data
  • We do not sell your personal data

5. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary to provide the app and comply with legal obligations.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data

To do so, contact us below.

7. Children’s Privacy

MyBite is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page.
